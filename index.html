Welcome to
Beaconsfield Elite Academy

Welcome to Beaconsfield Elite Academy which works with the complete child. Our aim is that every child that comes through the door is not only treated as special but feels special.

Our individual child centered approach encourages our children to excel in all areas (Academically, socially, physically and emotionally). Our experienced and dedicated teachers consistently set targets for every child with structured programme delivering the perfect mixture of care and learning helps your child to reach their fullest potentials.

At Beaconsfield Elite Academy we believe that our curriculum should be broad, balanced and relevant and meet the needs of all children whatever their ability. The taught curriculum is comprised of The National Curriculum and the wider curriculum and meets statutory requirements.

We ensure the children have a range of learning experiences that challenge, stimulate and promote thinking and learning through our creative curriculum which is based on first-hand experience. This encompasses all foundation subjects and enables us to provide a high standard of teaching and learning.

BEA is already equipped with a standard/functional ICT base and science laboratory, well planned/spacious classrooms with computers and projectors installed in each, sickbay, a library, play ground and a wide range of educational resources. All these are run by a team of experienced and dedicated staff who work under the auspices of experts in the field of education.
